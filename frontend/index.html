Placing Bets

Each match has 3 options — Team 1, Draw, or Team 2 — each with its own live odds. A draw only pays if you picked Draw.

You can change your pick, stake, or cancel any time until betting closes (5 min before kickoff). Your odds lock in when you bet — if they move, tap Adjust to switch your whole stake to the new odds.

Understanding Your Stats

R:R (Risk : Reward) — how much you aim to win per point staked, averaged across all your bets. A bet of 100 at 2.5x risks 100 to win 150, so R:R = 1.5:1. Low R:R means you back safe favourites; high R:R means you chase big underdogs.

ROI (Return on Investment) — how much you've actually made or lost. It's your total winnings minus total staked, divided by total staked. Stake 10,000 across bets and get back 11,500? That's a +15% ROI. Get back 8,000? That's −20%. R:R shows your style; ROI shows if it's working.
